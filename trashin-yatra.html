Trashin Yatra

You donate, we deliver

Help the needed once today

We are conducting an E-waste, Footwear, Clothing, and Books donation drive across Bangalore. If you have any items to donate, please fill out the form and an entirely non-contact pickup will be coordinated for you, right from your doorstep, through Trashin within a few days.
The collected E-Waste will be sent over to be repaired, after which all functioning devices, along with the donated books, will be passed over to government schools through Whitefield
For the Footwear and clothing drive, all items collected will be redirected to Rotary Indiranagar and BGMS, from where they will end up in the hands of those in need.
